Here is one mermaid diagram:

  graph RL
  A[Client] --> B[Load Balancer]
  B --> C[Server1]
  B --> D[Server2]
And here is another: 
  graph RL
  A[Client] -->|tcp_123| B
  B(Load Balancer)
  B -->|tcp_456| C[Server1]
  B -->|tcp_456| D[Server2]

Ordering: The diagram is specified in order of access. But we render right-to-left "RL" to indicate the order in which we build things. The mental model is that things are coming in from the right and, at first, building up the foundational blocks, and then eventually reaches a steady state of no blocks and data only.
